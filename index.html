OUR STORY

By Renters, for Renters

We understand the pain of looking for a new rental in San Diego. Finding the perfect place just a few blocks from the beach, paying the application fee, and then finding out that there are eight people ahead of you! With jobs to work, it's impossible to stay on rental websites all day looking for the newest postings.

Through our network of proprietary web scrapers, we find out about San Diego's newest rentals within 10 minutes of them being posted. We focus on local property managers - so if you're looking to rent in a building with 60 other units, our solution might not be for you. Our database will be filled primarily with multi-family homes, condos, apartments, and some single-family homes.

Using Rent Condor, you can find your dream place and apply before others see the lisitng. You'll end up at the top of the application list!
